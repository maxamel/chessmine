The Chessmine Philosophy

The main goal of the project is to provide a lightweight, hassle-free and minimalist chess experience while keeping it free and open for everyone. No registrations and no hidden fees. Just hop in, play a game, analyze it on the spot and go about your business. We don't save your games and analyses and you don't have to worry about registering or setting up and maintaining an account.

As infrastructure cloud costs are going up, it becomes harder and harder for developers to put up their free services online. Lichess yearly costs as of 2024 reached over 500K$. For this reason Chessmine is focused on minimalism and efficiency. There is always a fine balance between simplicity and enjoyability. Sometimes it is better to keep on the simple side of things, and not get into a feature creep labyrinth which ultimately only hurts user experience, overly complicates the software and soars the infrastructure costs through the roof.
